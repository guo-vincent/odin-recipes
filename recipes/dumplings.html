Dumpling Recipe

Required Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup flour: holds ingredients together.
  • 2 teaspoon baking powder: keeps dumplings light and fluffy.
  • 1 teaspoon sugar: adds hint of sweetness and helps dumplings retain moisture.
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt: enhances the flavor of the dumplings.
  • 1 tablespoon butter: binds dry ingredients together.
  • 1/2 cup milk: creates a smooth moist dough.

Directions

  1. Stir together flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt in a bowl.
  2. Cut and mix in butter until mixture is crumbly. Stir in milk and mix until batter forms that is thick enough to be scooped with a spoon. Let batter rest for 3 to 5 minutes.
  3. Drop batter by spoonfuls into boiling stew or soup. Cover and simmer without lifting the lid for 15 minutes. Serve.
  4. Enjoy!
