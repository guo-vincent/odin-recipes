Recipe:

Required Ingredients:

  • Meat: italian sausage or lean ground beef recommended.
  • Onion: adds good flavor.
  • Garlic: for the same reasons as adding onions.
  • Tomato products: For the tomato sauce.
  • Sugar: Adds subtle sweetness. You will need two tablespoons.
  • Spices: Fresh parsley, basil leabes, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper can all be used.
  • Lasagna noodles: Home-made is best, but store-brought works too.
  • Cheeses Any cheese will do.
  • Egg: To stabilize the ricotta.

How to make Lasagna

Here's a brief overview:
  1. Make the sauce for the meat.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let lasagna rest before serving.

Layering Lasagna

  1. Meat sauce
  2. Noodles
  3. Ricotta mixture
  4. Mozzarella slices
  5. Meat sauce
  6. Parmesan cheese
  7. Repeat as needed, topping up with Parmesan cheese

Cooking time

Lasagna, once assembled, should take around 50 minutes to cook in an oven preheated to 375 degrees F. Cover the lasagna with foil for the first 25 minutes, then let the lasagna cook uncovered for another 25 minutes. Also, let the lasagna rest at room temperature for about 15 minutes before serving.
There you have it! Your lasagna.
