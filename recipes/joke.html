Ingredients

  • 1 cup of Martian sand (extra fine, with a hint of red)
  • 2 tablespoons of liquid moonbeam extract
  • 3 slices of Saturn's ring dust (crushed for texture)
  • 1 can of fermented Venusian fog
  • 1 cup of Jupiter mashed cloud fluff (harvested at high altitudes)
  • 1/2 cup of pulverized comet ice
  • 4 ounces of neutron star cheese (so good it'll pull you to it!)
  • 3 strands of Andromeda galaxy tinsel (for intergalactic shimmer)
  • 2 tablespoons of dark matter syrup
  • 1 handful of asteroid sprinkles (assorted sizes)
  • 2 shots of pure solar flare juice (handle with care)
  • 1/2 cup of shredded comsmic string theory (extra springy!)
  • 1 tablespoon of liquified black hole (to keep everything together!)
  • 1 cup of stardust infused antimatter foam
  • 3 pints of concentrated gamma ray bursts

Instructions

  1. Martian Base: add martian sand with liquid moonbeam extract into a zero gravity-mixing bowl. Stir until the mixture is glowing.
  2. Add rings and fog: sprinkle in the crushed Saturn's ring dust, followed by a generous splash of fermented Venusian fog. The mix should start to shimmer.
  3. Jovian Cloud Fluff: gently fold in the mashed cloud fluff from Jupiter. Don't let it evaporate. Also add the gamma ray bursts.
  4. Comet Ice Crunch: Add pulverized comet ice, cooling the mixture and giving it and otherworldly refreshing chill.
  5. Neutron Star Cheese: Carefully place the neutron star cheese in the center. You should be drawn in by the dish at this point.
  6. Tinsel and Dark Matter: Interweace the Andromeda Galaxy tinsel throughout the mix. Then drizzle with dark matter syrup for a burst of mysterious flavor.
  7. Asteroid sprinkles: Toss in asteroid sprinkles, giving the concoction an extra-terrestial crunch.
  8. Solar flare kick: Pour in the solar flare uice with extreme caution fo a fiery explosive kick!
  9. Black hole twist: Add a drop of black hole. The dish should start swirling if it hasnt already.
  10. Antimatter foam: top the dish with stardust-infused antimatter foam for a light, frothy finish that defies the laws of physics.
  11. Meteorite Garnish: Arrange slices of meteorite-encrusted space sponge around the edges, completing your cosmic creation.
